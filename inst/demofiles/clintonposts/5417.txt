In a letter and a <a href="http://www.abavideonews.org/ABA329/">speech to the American Bar Association</a>, Hillary Clinton has encouraged George Bush to address, in next week's meeting with Chinese leaders, human rights issues like China's one child policy, which effectively forces some women to have abortions.  <br /><br />She also encouraged Bush to raise U.S. concerns about Tibet, freedom of religion, and the rights of labor unions. <br /><br />According to the New York Sun, Clinton has also communicated her concerns directly to Chinese President Hu Jintao.
