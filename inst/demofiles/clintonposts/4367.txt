<p>A judge in Los Angeles yesterday dismissed Sen. Hillary Clinton from a lawsuit by business mogul <a href="http://www.peterfpaul.com">Peter Franklin Paul</a> that alleges her husband, former President Bill Clinton, reneged on a $17 million business deal. </p>

<p>President Clinton however, remains a defendant and will be subpoenaed early next week to testify in a deposition. A trial date has been set, and Paul plans to depose Sen. Clinton as well. </p>

<p>Represented by the public-interest law firm <a href="http://www.usjf.net/">U.S. Justice Foundation</a>, Paul claims Bill Clinton agreed to promote Paul's Internet businesses after leaving office in exchange for his financial backing of a Hollywood gala and fund-raiser for Sen. Clinton's Senate campaign in 2000.</p>
