<p>My gosh.  Is it that late?  Where have I been all morning?  In a meeting.  With the Boss.  Ugly stuff.  I spent the first hour wondering exactly who the hell this shiny penny in the fancy suit was &#8211; and why the hell he would drag me into a room so early in the morning.  <a href="http://www.defamer.com/hollywood/upload/2006/07/lohan-tanbikini.jpg">Sexual harassment</a> papers were at the ready.  The next couple hours were spent in forced contemplation of the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sirens_of_titan">chrono-synclastic infundibulum</a> something or other and how we might be best served with a cross amortization in regard to market share and a spectral line of synergistic alpha generators when spaced out by equal lengths of green colored paper.  Mostly, he was a cheerleader.  Sometimes we raised the roof.  With a whimper, it was over.</p>

	<p>But that doesn&rsquo;t mean the world wasn&rsquo;t spinning all day, or that people weren&rsquo;t joyfully killing each other, or that Britney Spears inexplicably continues to become a <a href="http://www.cnn.com/2006/TECH/internet/08/23/google.trends.ap/index.html">declining commodity</a> on Google Trends.  No, the beat marched on.  And I catch up.</p>
