<p>I believe that creative, unique, and numerous blog categories are an effective method for <em>gaming the blogosphere</em>. More <em>is</em> better.<a id="more-110"></a></p>
<div class="entry-more" id="more">I noticed a while ago that <a href="http://www.chrisabraham.com/2005/11/having_your_own.html">Wonkette has a category for me</a>, <a href="http://www.wonkette.com/politics/chris-abraham/index.php">/chris-abraham/</a>.</p>
<p><em>Wonkette is gaming the blogosphere!</em></p>
<p>I wondered why until I watched <a href="http://www.extrememortman.com/about-extreme-mortman/">Howard Mortman</a> go nuts with the categories on <a href="http://extrememortman.com/">Extreme Mortman</a>. He is the most whimsical category-maker on the planet and I think this will benefit his blog immensely.</p>
<p><em>Howard Mortman is copying Wonkette!</em></p>
