<br />A new Iowa Poll conducted for <em>The Des Moines Register</em> shows that Edwards, the runner-up in the Iowa Democratic caucuses two years ago and a frequent visitor to the state since then, is the choice of 30 percent of Iowans who say they are likely to take part in the January 2008 caucuses.<br />
