<p>I get inspired by so many things I see in stores, on tv, online, and in life.</p>
<p><a href="http://weewonderfuls.typepad.com/">Wee Wonderfuls</a> is such a place.  Hillary makes adorable things and it&#8217;s all I can do not squee uncontrollably when I&#8217;m browsing her blog.  I came across her <a href="http://weewonderfuls.typepad.com/wee_wonderfuls/2005/03/shrinky_dink_pi.html">lily pad pin cushion</a> and knew I had to make one for myself.</p>
<p>It&#8217;s rare that I&#8217;ll copy something exactly.  But the colors and style of it just worked so well, that there wasn&#8217;t anything I could do to make it better.  So to make it &#8220;mine&#8221; I changed the shank button from pearl to pink.  Yay for individuality!  Hah!</p>
