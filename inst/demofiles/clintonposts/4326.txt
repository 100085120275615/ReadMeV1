	<p><p>It drizzled when we &#8216;ran&#8217; to the Hoe. Arrived a bit late &#8211; 15mins before the first blast. Much packed compared to yesterday. Lots of people  brought along poncho, chair &amp; tents. Even the grassy slope towards Madeira Rd was full of people. The atmosphere was awesome. I reach to my exact same spot as yesterday cos arriving late, had not much choice of location to shoot though. I had something handy this time, CJ lend me his tripod to shoot.</p></p>

	<p><p>I placed my tripod on the Citadel wall ruin &#8211; it was uneven surface but luckily the tripod was retractable up to 3 levels. After getting the tripod balanced, I mounted the cam &amp; tune to my settings. The spectators in front of me was so cooperative enough to have stand further so that they are not obstructing my view (thanks!)</p></p>
